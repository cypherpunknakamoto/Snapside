# Privacy Policy for SnapSide

**Last Updated: November 15, 2025**

## Data Collection and Privacy

SnapSide is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our data practices:

### No Data Collection
- **SnapSide does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
- All screenshots and OCR processing happen **locally on your Mac**.
- No information is sent to external servers.

### No Tracking
- We do not use analytics, tracking SDKs, or third-party advertising.
- We do not track your usage, behavior, or activities.
- We do not collect device information, location data, or any identifiable information.

### Local Processing Only
- All features, including screenshot capture and text recognition, are processed entirely on your device.
- Your screenshots and extracted text remain private and under your control.
- No cloud storage or external services are utilized.

### Third-Party Services
- SnapSide does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that would access your data.

### Data Security
- Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app.

### Changes to This Policy
- If we ever change our data practices, we will update this policy and notify users through app updates.

### Contact
- For questions about this privacy policy, please visit: https://github.com/cypherpunknakamoto/snapside

---

**Summary:** SnapSide respects your privacy completely. Everything stays on your Mac. Zero data collection. Zero tracking. Zero transmission.
